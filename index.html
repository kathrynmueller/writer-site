As a freelance writer with over a decade of professional experience writing copy, articles, and business communications, I specialize in working with small businesses and nonprofit organizations to create a fresh, professional, and engaging voice for written communications. From my earliest days I've written stories, and I kept writing through college at Brown University and grad school at The University of Arizona. Now my favorite stories to tell are nonfiction -- from articles, press releases and travel itineraries, to blog postings and Facebook status updates. I also build web sites (e.g. this one!) and set up accounts for social media and email marketing services.

I'm also a Grammy-nominated soprano and a budding web developer. I live in Raleigh, North Carolina, where I enjoy the year-round Farmers Market offerings and spending time with my husband, two tiny children, and crazy dog.
